Identity Eternal

Where your identity is truly yours everywhere

What it is

Identity Eternal to offer a user convenient means of making a call through another user's line by showing the first users identity will reduce call rejection, enable more calls and generate more income.

Objectives

  • To minimize call rejections
  • To save distress period of flat phone battery, when phone is damaged or no phone credit
  • To save situations and time
  • Prevent awkward removal of sim card from one phone to another

What it will solve

Accumulation of rejected calls lead to revenue lost to mobile networks and there are lots of issues not solved.

Details

Allows user A mobile identity ( phone number) to be seen whenever he/she is in distress of no phone credit, flat phone battery or when phone is damaged through user B phone without necessarily swapping SIM Cards. This is achieved by dialling a code, own mobile number followed by recipient's number on user B phone. The recipient thereby sees User A number instead of User B phone number. It is also to work across different mobile networks especially any is unavailable at a particular place or time.

features:

  • Continuous connectivity
  • One's sim card or phone may not be necessarily carried about
  • Mobile identity is continuous
  • No awkward removal of sim from one phone to another
  • Secured service as it will have security pin/password
  • Power outage no longer a problem
  • Minimize call rejection
  • Generates more income as there are no limits to call making
  • Saves time, life and situations/issues
  • Platform for productivity and success
  • More calls ....

Description as registered with the patent office:

For Person A mobile identity (phone line) be seen whenever he or she is in depress of no phone charge (credit) or no phone battery or when his or her phone is damaged, through Person B phone line without removing Person A Sim Card and inserted in person B phone. Access to be provided by Mobile Networks by allowing Person A dial a code, own security code, own mobile line, then recipient number inside Person B phone line and Send, thereby the recipient sees Person A number instead of Person B phone number. It is also to work whenever network A service is unavailable at a particular place or time, network B or C will grant access for Person A mobile identity (phone number) be seen through Person B phone line.
